3 Pain Points of Traditional Booking

1. Missed Calls = Lost Customers

Beauticians can't answer the phone while doing facials, clinics have no one answering during lunch breaks, and restaurants are too busy during peak hours to reply on LINE. Every missed call is a potentially lost order. New customers who can't reach you typically just go elsewhere.

2. Double Booking

Using paper records or memory to manage time slots easily leads to scheduling two groups of customers in the same slot. The result: long waits, poor experiences, and bad reviews. A LINE booking system locks booked slots in real-time, preventing double bookings at the root.

3. No-Shows

Customers who book but don't show up are every appointment-based business's nightmare. Each empty slot is lost revenue. The traditional approach is calling to confirm, but that's labor-intensive. A LINE booking system sends automatic reminders before appointments, drastically reducing no-shows.

Real Case: A Taipei beauty studio saw monthly bookings increase from 80 to 120 after implementing a LINE booking system, with no-show rates dropping from 20% to 5%. The owner no longer needs to spend time answering calls and replying to messages, freeing up 50 extra hours per month to focus on services.

How Does the LINE Booking System Work?

The entire booking flow can be completed within the LINE chat — no need to redirect to external websites:

  1. Customer adds LINE official account: Scan QR Code or search the account name
  2. Tap the "Book" button: Triggered via Rich Menu or keyword
  3. Select service type: System displays bookable services (e.g., haircut, perm, facial)
  4. Select date and time slot: Only available slots are shown, preventing double bookings
  5. Confirm booking: Fill in name and phone number; system immediately replies with booking confirmation
  6. Auto reminders: Reminder messages sent 24 hours and 1 hour before the appointment
  7. Backend management: All booking records synced to Google Sheets or backend system

Which Industries Are Best Suited for LINE Booking?

Beauty Salons / Nail Art / Eyelash Extensions

Stylists usually can't answer phones while providing services. A LINE booking system lets customers choose their stylist and time slot on their own, with service options (wash & cut, perm, color, etc.). Combined with booking reminders that significantly reduce no-shows, it's one of the highest-ROI industries.

Clinics / Dentists / Traditional Chinese Medicine

Medical appointments require precise time slot management. The LINE booking system can auto-schedule based on doctor consultation timetables, supporting different durations for first visits vs. follow-ups, specialty selection, and automatic pre-appointment reminders. It reduces front desk burden and improves patient experience.

Restaurants / Cafes

Reservation features plus party size, special requests (birthday celebrations, high chairs), and auto waitlist notifications. During peak hours, you won't need to answer the phone, so service quality doesn't drop due to taking reservation calls.

Gyms / Yoga Studios / Fitness Classes

Class bookings, trainer schedule management, and automatic capacity control. Students can view the weekly schedule and remaining spots directly on LINE, booking or canceling instantly.
